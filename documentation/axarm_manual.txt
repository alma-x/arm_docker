### STARTUP COMMAND

FOR TEST DRIVE ENVIRONMENT:
roslaunch ur3_control TD2.launch

FOR SIMULATION:
roslaunch ur3_control init.launch inter_arg:=1
(actually TD2.launch it's only a convinience copy of this one)

### OBJECTIVE COMMANDS

1)	roslaunch ur3_control objective1.launch


2)	roslaunch ur3_control objective2.launch input:="ID1 ID2 ID3 ID4"

	SUBSTITUTE ID1,... WITH A INTEGER NUMBER FOR EXAMPLE input:="1 2 10 12"
	

3)	roslaunch ur3_control objective3.launch

4)	roslaunch ur3_control objective4.launch angle:="ANGLE"

	SUBSTITUTE ANGLE WITH A FLOAT NUMBER FOR EXAMPLE angle:="12.50"
	

5)	roslaunch ur3_control objective5.launch

6)	roslaunch ur3_control objective6.launch

7)	roslaunch ur3_control objective7.launch

8)	roslaunch ur3_control objective8.launch

9)	roslaunch ur3_control objective9.launch {tag:=$(rosparam get hidden_id)} OR tag:=ID_h

	NOTE THAT IF NOTHING IS SPECIFIED, PROGRAM WILL RECOVER HIDDEN ID ON ITS OWN
	OTHERWISE SUBSTITUTE ID WITH A INTEGER NUMBER FOR EXAMPLE tag:="1"


10)	roslaunch ur3_control objective10.launch


### TERMINATION
roslaunch ur3_control kill_node.launch

OR just hit Ctrl+D until outside container and kill it
